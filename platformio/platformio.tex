\section*{GATE CS 2019 – Question 6 Analysis}

\textbf{Question:}

In 16-bit 2’s complement representation, the decimal number $-28$ is:

(A) 1111 1111 0001 1100  
(B) 0000 0000 1110 0100  
(C) 1111 1111 1110 0100  
(D) 1000 0000 1110 0100  

\vspace{0.2cm}
\hrule
\vspace{0.3cm}

\textbf{Step 1: Convert +28 to Binary}

\[
28_{10} = 11100_2
\]

16-bit form:

\[
0000\ 0000\ 0001\ 1100
\]

\textbf{Step 2: 1’s Complement}

\[
1111\ 1111\ 1110\ 0011
\]

\textbf{Step 3: Add 1}

\[
1111\ 1111\ 1110\ 0011
+
0000\ 0000\ 0000\ 0001
=
\mathbf{1111\ 1111\ 1110\ 0100}
\]

\textbf{Correct Option: (C)}
